# The purpose of this blog

I’m seeking to grow in faith, fervor, and understanding. I will mostly (but not exclusively) write about Catholic themes, such as what I’ve learned from Bible readings or my experiences trying to live a Christian life. I also want to practice writing in Japanese about my thoughts and interests in a way that is genuine, even if what I write is technically poor. Where I write in both English and Japanese, the intention is not necessarily to have equivalent translations side-by-side, but rather direct production in the working language.

Two principals restricting my posts:

  1. No images
  2. No machine translation

時々日本語で書く予定です。GoogleTranslateを使わず頑張りたいと思います。一つの理由は思想的ですけど、本当の目標は、日本語の練習と自分の考えを自然に伝えることです。多分ほぼ会話っぽい感じになると思います；今までは書くと読む体験があまりないですから「本当に良くないと分かるよ」。というわけで、変な日本語が出てきてすみません。
